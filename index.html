About

Built from scratch.
By one person.
For one reason.

I built this platform — every route, every query, every surface — because the tools that existed for independent restaurants were not good enough. Not in features, not in ownership, and not in economics.

I spent years reading restaurant revenue data as a data scientist — peak hours, margin patterns, order trends. The same gap appeared in every dataset: great food, real customers, and a digital infrastructure that captured almost none of the revenue it could generate. The customer data went to Zomato. The brand lived as a thumbnail. The catering enquiry went to WhatsApp and got lost.

Kravon is the operating system built to close that gap. One platform. Every channel. Your customer data. No commission. No ongoing dependency on me.

Nikhil Tengle Founder · Kravon · Mumbai

I built the entire platform — backend, database, every surface — because the tools that existed were not good enough. The backend is Node.js on Railway. The database is PostgreSQL. The ordering, dine-in, catering, and analytics are all live. I did this after years of reading restaurant revenue data as a data scientist and watching the same gap appear in every dataset. The platform is the product. I am now onboarding the first restaurants directly.

Belief 01 · No Commission

The commission model is a tax on loyalty. Every customer who reorders through an aggregator costs 18–30% — forever. A direct channel pays for itself. Kravon makes that channel permanent.

Belief 02 · Ownership

Your customer data belongs to you. Every order, every customer, every catering lead — yours. Not Zomato's. Not Kravon's. The restaurant's.

Belief 03 · One Platform

A restaurant should not need five tools to operate online. Presence, ordering, dine-in, catering, intelligence — one login, one config, one bill.
